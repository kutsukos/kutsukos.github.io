Μαύρη είναι η νύχτα στα βουνά: Μια ταινία μικρού μήκους με λύκους [movie]


Μαύρη είναι η νύχτα στα βουνά: Μια ταινία μικρού μήκους με λύκους is the first act of a 3-acts movie.
This act was shot in Crete, Greece, a place without wolves on the mountains. Produced and edited in HealthyFoodStudio_2k18, a place full of healthy food.
Warning: This video has been identified to potentially trigger seizures for people with photosensitive epilepsy. Viewer discretion is advised!
