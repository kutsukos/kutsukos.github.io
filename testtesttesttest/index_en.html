About us

Forest Products S.A. is a timber trading agency in Athens, Greece, operating for over 40 years in the Greek timber market, working in close liaison with Theodoridis AB of Stockholm, Sweden.
We began our operations representing sawmills from Scandinavia, and in the following years gradually expanded our business sourcing timber and wood products from North America, Europe, South America, Asia and Africa.
Our target markets are Greece, Albania, Romania and other Balkan countries, while our mother company, Theodoridis AB, operates also in the Middle East and North Africa markets.
