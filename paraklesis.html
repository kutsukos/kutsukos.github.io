Paraklesis

Paraklesis by Ormylia, an ecclesiastical byzantine music album, is what we are listening on radio while going to Panagia Kaliviani's monastery, every 15th of August, on Assumption of Virgin Mary day. A Paraklesis (Greek: Παράκλησις), is a service of supplication for the welfare of the living.

Panagia Kaliviani's monastery is a womens' monastery located 5km from Moires village and closeby to village of Kalivia, which is why it has named Panagia (Virgin Mary) Kaliviani. source: www.greeka.com

I found my self participating in this celebration two years in a row and liking it, though I do not believe in god. This made me to create this project, to show you what I am seeing there every year and to create a more complete view, about this monastery, year after year.

In 2017, I used my phone and in 2018 I tried to use my DSLR camera, to take photos. I wish I will have more confidence and "rudeness", to take more pictures, in the next years.
